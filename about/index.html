Overthinking
The thoughts that escaped my head... by Spencer Haan

Hi, I'm Spencer, and I'm an overthinker.

Ever hear, "I think you're overthinking this"? It's something I hear a lot. It often gets me into trouble.. with myself.. as I take unlikely scenarios and make them even more unlikely. Without fail. Time and time again.

It's been a source of anxiety for me for as long as I can remember. It's also often caused by anxiety, or perhaps just made worse by it. This creates a sort of whirlpool of anxiety, feeding into itself and spiraling downwards. Well, at least until I can figure out what it is that's bothering me.. or until I accept that I can't figure it out.

When I was younger a lot of this anxiety came in the form of preparing myself for worst case scenarios. That howling wind outside? Could be a tornado. If it is I'd need to make sure to grab my favourite possessions - the ones my young mind couldn't live without - wake everyone up, and make sure we didn't forget to bring the dog or hamsters with us into the cellar.

Every so often I'll obsess over different ideas, concepts, and so on, asking myself what it means or why it is the way that it is. I'll frequently come back to these ideas and concepts, like a kid grabbing a toy out of a toybox, and continue to think about them some more. Sometimes I'll do research to further my understanding of an idea or concept, other times I'll just stay in my head just to enjoy the process of thinking.

I figured this blog could be a way to let some of those thoughts out. Perhaps even allow me to further understand those thoughts as a requirement to articulate them. It may also allow me to build some confidence in myself, and perhaps be more decivisve with my words. It's kind of scary putting something out into the world, exposing it to scrutiny and criticism, especially when you're used to the comfort of your own mind.

Posts will likely be infrequent and not on any kind of schedule. I doubt there will be a specific theme, just whatever it is I'm thinking about at the time that I think is worth writing about.
